# Recioto della Valpolicella Classico, Corte Sant'Alda, 2018

## Quick tasting profile
Deep ruby-garnet with purple highlights (younger and more vivid than Amarone, less dry); concentrated nose of preserved cherry, black cherry jam, fig, dark chocolate, violet and sweet spice — the sweet counterpart to Amarone's dry power; sweet and luscious on the palate (unlike the technically dry Amarone) with firm but sweet tannins; full-bodied (15% ABV), concentrated, with a velvety, warming finish. The sweetness and lack of the bitter finish distinguishes Recioto from Amarone at the key moment of palate assessment.

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Corvina Veronese (45-95%), Rondinella (5-30%), other approved varieties
- Vineyard: Valpolicella Classico zone (the historic core — Negrar, Marano, Fumane, Sant'Ambrogio, San Pietro in Cariano); Corte Sant'Alda is a biodynamic estate in Mezzane di Sotto
- Vinification: Appassimento — same grape drying process as Amarone (3-4 months); but fermentation is arrested before dryness, leaving residual sugar; Recioto is Amarone's sweet "parent"
- Elevage: Old oak casks typical; DOCG requires minimum aging (Source: common knowledge — specific regulation Source needed)
- ABV / RS / TA: 15% / sweet (RS ≥ approximately 50-80 g/L) / medium acidity

## Vintage character (2018 Valpolicella)
2018 was a warm, ripe vintage in Veneto with excellent fruit quality for appassimento production. The dried grapes of 2018 show concentrated fruit with good sugar levels, producing rich, intensely flavoured Recioto. Source needed for specific 2018 Valpolicella harvest report.

## Producer style
Corte Sant'Alda is a small biodynamic estate in Mezzane di Sotto, Valpolicella. Run by Marinella Camerani, the estate is one of the more quality-focused and respected smaller Valpolicella producers, known for traditional grape drying and biodynamic principles. Their wines typically show more freshness and terroir character than large commercial Amarone/Recioto producers. Source needed for specific estate acreage and biodynamic certification.
