# Malbec Reserve Old Vine, Altocedro, 2018

## Quick tasting profile
This should be the darker, more muscular Malbec of the pair, with black fruit, spice, mocha and firmer tannic grip. The old-vine and reserve cues point to a more concentrated, oak-shaped and ageworthy style than Zuccardi Serie A.

## Vintage character (2018 Uco Valley)
2018 in Mendoza offered balance and freshness, and in stronger sites produced complete, well-structured reds rather than overripe wines. That suits a reserve-style Malbec, allowing concentration without sacrificing definition.

## Producer style
Altocedro is a quality-focused Uco Valley producer with a reputation for structured, site-expressive reds. The Old Vine Reserve bottling is positioned above simple category Malbec, aiming for more depth, oak integration and cellarworthiness.
