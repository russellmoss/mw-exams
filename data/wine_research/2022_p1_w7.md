# Gewurztraminer, Erste+Neue, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
This should be unmistakably aromatic: rose petal, lychee, ginger and exotic spice, but in Alto Adige form it should stay more structured and less overtly sweet than many Alsace examples. The palate is likely broad and perfumed, yet dry enough to keep the finish savory and food-oriented.

## Vintage character (2020 Alto Adige)
2020 favored healthy, expressive aromatic whites in many northern Italian areas, and Alto Adige retained the diurnal range needed for perfume and acidity. In Gewurztraminer, that usually means generous floral and tropical aromas with enough mountain freshness to stop the wine from becoming blowsy.

## Producer style
Erste+Neue was a prominent Alto Adige cooperative centered on Kaltern/Caldaro, with Gewurztraminer among its important white varieties. The style expectation is polished and varietally clear rather than radically artisanal, making it a strong representative of premium but commercially legible Alto Adige aromatics.
