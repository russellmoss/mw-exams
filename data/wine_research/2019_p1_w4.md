# Sauvignon Blanc, The Doctors', 2017

## Quick tasting profile
Very pale lemon with lifted elderflower, lime, gooseberry and fresh herbs. The palate is light-bodied and intensely aromatic, but noticeably lower in alcohol than a standard Marlborough Sauvignon Blanc. A touch of residual sugar softens the sharp acid line, giving a light, zesty, early-drinking style.

## Producer style
The Doctors' is a specialist Marlborough label built around naturally lighter wines, particularly lower-alcohol Sauvignon Blanc. The commercial proposition is clear: preserve the region's pungent aromatics and crisp refreshment while offering a softer, more sessionable style than mainstream Marlborough examples.
