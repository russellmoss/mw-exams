# Wally Chenin Blanc, Les Caves de Loire, 2019

## Quick tasting profile
Pale to medium gold; fresh and straightforward nose of green apple, pear, citrus zest and white flowers — unoaked entry-level Anjou Chenin character with quince and mild floral notes. Medium body (12.5% ABV) with Chenin Blanc's naturally high acidity driving a clean, crisp mouthfeel; dry style with minimal residual sugar; medium finish. Contrast to the grand terroir expressions of Savennières (w6 in this paper); this is the commercial, approachable end of Loire Chenin.

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: 100% Chenin Blanc
- Vineyard: Anjou AOC; schist and volcanic soils typical of the Layon/Anjou corridor
- Vinification: Stainless steel fermentation; early harvested for freshness; no malo likely
- Elevage: Tank-aged; early bottling for freshness
- ABV / RS / TA: 12.5% / dry / Source needed

## Vintage character (2019 Anjou)
2019 was a warm, early vintage across the Loire with very ripe fruit; excellent conditions for Chenin Blanc with good natural sugars and retained acidity due to cool nights. Dry Anjou Chenin in 2019 shows more generosity and riper citrus/stone-fruit character than the naturally lean style of cooler years. An accessible, commercially appealing vintage.

## Producer style
Les Caves de Loire is a cooperative / négociant based in Brissac-Loire-Aubance, Anjou, producing large volumes of commercially positioned Loire wines under various labels. "Wally" is a branded range targeting everyday drinking at accessible price points — a mass-market interpretation of Anjou Chenin Blanc aimed at on-trade and supermarket channels.
