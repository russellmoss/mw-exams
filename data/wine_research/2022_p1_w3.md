# Riesling Springvale, Grosset, 2021

## Quick tasting profile
Bone-dry, intensely limey Clare Riesling with floral lift, chalky tension and no oak signature. Compared with Germanic benchmarks it should feel more solar and textural through the middle, but the finish stays rigid, stony and long.

## Vintage character (2021 Clare Valley)
2021 is generally regarded as a very strong Australian vintage, with good natural acidity and flavor concentration after a cooler, more even growing season than the preceding hot drought years. For Clare Riesling that usually means purity, fine line and cellaring potential rather than broadness.

## Producer style
Grosset is one of Australia's benchmark Riesling producers and farms Springvale as certified organic and biodynamic estate fruit. Springvale is distinguished by its Watervale red loam-over-limestone profile and is often the more floral, mid-palate-generous counterpart to the stricter Polish Hill style in the Grosset range.
