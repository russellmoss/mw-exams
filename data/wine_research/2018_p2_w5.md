# Baron de Ley Gran Reserva, 2011

## Quick tasting profile
Ruby with some garnet at the rim, showing dried red cherry, coconut, cedar, vanilla, leather, and sweet spice. The palate is dry, medium-bodied, and smooth-textured, with polished tannin and the mature American-oak-derived notes typical of traditional Rioja.
