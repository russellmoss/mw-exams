# Pinot Gris, Charles Schleret, 2007

## Quick tasting profile
Deepening straw-gold with clear bottle development. Expect ripe pear, quince, honeyed spice, smoke, and a broad palate shape; the alcohol and glycerol weight are likely more obvious than sharp acidity, making this a richer, textural Alsace white rather than a nervy one.
