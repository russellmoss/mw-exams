# Cava Brut Reserva Essential Púrpura, Juvé y Camps, 2021

## Quick tasting profile
Pale lemon with persistent mousse. Blind, this should show traditional-method Cava markers of apple, white flowers and light toast, but with a comparatively generous, rounded palate and evident but moderate dosage rather than the stricter, bone-dry feel of many Brut Nature bottlings (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Juve%CC%81-Camps-ESSENTIAL-PU%CC%81RPURA-Ficha-Te%CC%81cnica.pdf).

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Xarel·lo, Macabeo and Parellada (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Juve%CC%81-Camps-ESSENTIAL-PU%CC%81RPURA-Ficha-Te%CC%81cnica.pdf)
- Vineyard: Espiells plus long-term partner growers in Penedès (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Essential-Purpura-Cava-Juve-y-Camps-ENG.pdf)
- Soils: Loam-clay-limestone on the English technical sheet for Essential Púrpura (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Essential-Purpura-Cava-Juve-y-Camps-ENG.pdf)
- Vinification: Varieties fermented separately in stainless steel; malolactic fermentation completed before blending; second fermentation in bottle (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Juve%CC%81-Camps-ESSENTIAL-PU%CC%81RPURA-Ficha-Te%CC%81cnica.pdf)
- Elevage: Minimum 24 months on lees in bottle (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Juve%CC%81-Camps-ESSENTIAL-PU%CC%81RPURA-Ficha-Te%CC%81cnica.pdf)
- ABV / RS / TA: 12.0% / 8.6 g/L / 5.7 g/L (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Juve%CC%81-Camps-ESSENTIAL-PU%CC%81RPURA-Ficha-Te%CC%81cnica.pdf)

## Vintage character (2021 Penedès)
Juvé y Camps’ own Essential range materials describe 2021 as dry and warm, effectively the beginning of the current drought cycle, but with sufficient reserves to deliver wines with strong fruit character and notable ageing potential (source: https://shop.juveycamps.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/Ficha-essential-xarello-2021_A4_ENG.pdf). For this wine the result is ripe fruit within a disciplined traditional-method frame.

## Producer style
Juvé y Camps is one of the best-known long-established quality Cava houses, with an emphasis on bottle ageing, organic/regenerative viticulture and a polished house style. Essential Púrpura is positioned as the welcoming entry into that universe rather than the sternest terroir statement.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 4 starts the Spain trio in 2023 Paper 3, Question 2. It tests candidates on Cava method, Reserva ageing and the commercial positioning of a serious but still broadly marketable bottle-fermented sparkling wine.
