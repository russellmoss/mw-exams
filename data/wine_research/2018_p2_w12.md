# Cabernet Sauvignon, Vinedo Chadwick, 2014

## Quick tasting profile
Deep ruby with cassis, graphite, cedar, mint, and polished dark oak spice. The palate is dry, concentrated, and Cabernet-led in structure, with firm but refined tannin and the cool-Andean freshness that marks top Puente Alto.

## Vintage character (2014 Maipo)
2014 in Chile is generally seen as a fresher and more classical vintage than the hot 2015s that followed. In top Maipo Cabernet, that usually means aromatic precision, graphite, and better line through the palate.

## Producer style
Vinedo Chadwick is one of Chile's prestige Cabernet references, designed to compete in the global icon-wine category. The style is polished, ageworthy, and explicitly fine-wine rather than commercial.

## Why it's in this exam
This completed the Americas pair in 2018 paper 2. It gives the paper a high-end Chilean Cabernet benchmark against the more overtly ripe, varietal California Zinfandel.
