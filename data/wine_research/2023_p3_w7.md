# Cru Classé Côtes de Provence Rosé, Château Galoupet, 2023

## Quick tasting profile
Very pale but not trivial: rich summer fruit, yellow peach, floral notes and a saline, silky palate. Blind, this should suggest ambitious gastronomic Provence rosé rather than poolside simplicity, with more body, oak influence and ageing intent than the average category wine (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023).

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Grenache, Syrah, Tibouren, Rolle, Cinsault and Sémillon (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023)
- Vineyard: Estate plots in Côtes de Provence, managed on a parcel basis (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023)
- Soils: Exact soil series for this bottling were not stated on the page consulted. Source needed.
- Vinification: Plot-by-plot vinification in smaller vats; Tibouren handled to emphasize its yellow-fruit and saline profile (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023)
- Elevage: 40% of the wine vinified in 600L French oak demi-muids, 60% new and 40% one-year-old, then aged on fine lees for 4-5 months before final blending (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023)

## Vintage character (2023 Provence)
Galoupet describes 2023 as drastically dry, the least rainy year recorded on the estate since its weather station was installed, with low yields of about 28 hL/ha and concentrated berries (source: https://www.chateaugaloupet.com/en-ww/our-wines/chateau-galoupet-cru-classe-2023). That helps explain the wine’s depth and saline concentration despite its pale color.

## Producer style
Château Galoupet is positioning itself at the premium, gastronomic end of Provence rosé: certified organic, parcel-specific, biodiverse farming, premium packaging and deliberate use of oak. The rosé is sold as a fine-wine proposition with 3-4 years of ageing potential, not a disposable seasonal pink.
