# Old Vines Zinfandel, Ravenswood, 2014

## Quick tasting profile
Deep ruby-purple with jammy blackberry, black cherry, pepper, sweet spice, and often a touch of raisined fruit. The palate is dry to just-fruity in impression, full-bodied, warm, and plush, with moderate acidity and broad tannins.

## Vintage character (2014 Lodi)
2014 was a warm, drought-era California vintage, often producing ripe, concentrated reds with elevated generosity. In Lodi Zinfandel, that typically means high fruit sweetness, warmth, and broad market appeal.

## Producer style
Ravenswood is one of the best-known Zinfandel brands in the US, positioned at the intersection of varietal typicity and broad commercial recognition. The style is unapologetically Californian, ripe, and consumer-friendly.
