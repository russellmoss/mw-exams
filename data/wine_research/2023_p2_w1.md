# Barolo Ravera, Elvio Cogno, 2015

## Quick tasting profile
Classic Nebbiolo from a serious cru: pale to medium garnet, intensely perfumed, and structured by firm tannin rather than by color weight. Expect rose, red cherry, mint, tobacco and mineral notes, with a full but stern palate, high acidity and long, savory persistence that still feels youthful.

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Nebbiolo (Lampia and Michet sub-varieties)
- Vineyard: Ravera MGA in Novello, south-facing, around 380m
- Soils: mainly calcareous soils
- Vinification: temperature-controlled stainless steel with automatic pump-over; 30-day post-fermentation maceration with submerged cap
- Elevage: 24 months in large 25-30hl Slavonian oak barrels, then 6 months bottle age

## Vintage character (2015 Barolo)
Berry Bros. & Rudd's 2015 Barolo report characterizes the vintage as warm but successful, with ripe tannins and earlier accessibility than more severe years. That fits Ravera well: the wine should still be structured and ageworthy, but 2015 often gives more early generosity than a classically austere year. In exam terms, this is a useful reminder that a Barolo can be both serious and already expressive.

## Producer style
Elvio Cogno's official material presents Ravera as the historical cru of Novello and emphasizes classicism: long maceration, large Slavonian casks, no fining or filtration, and a focus on site over cellar flash. Secondary material on the estate also frames the family as specialists in Ravera, using different exposures and vine material within the cru to show nuance rather than chasing multiple famous communes. Commercially this is a benchmark traditionalist, premium Barolo.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 1 opens 2023 Paper 2 Question 1, where all four wines are Italian reds and candidates must identify origin, then discuss quality, maturity, ageing capacity and production. Ravera is the high-bar example: if a candidate cannot explain why calcareous Novello Nebbiolo plus long maceration plus large cask yields ageworthy structure, the rest of the set becomes harder to calibrate.
