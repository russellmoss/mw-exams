# Zinfandel Mother Clone, Pedroncelli, 2019

## Quick tasting profile
This should be ripe, spicy and distinctly Californian: jammy berry fruit, cherry, white pepper, licorice and vanilla with high alcohol and broad texture. It is generous and extrovert rather than restrained, but should still keep enough acidity to stay in balance.

## Vintage character (2019 Dry Creek Valley)
2019 in Sonoma generally gave generous but balanced ripe reds, and Dry Creek handled Zinfandel particularly well. That tends to produce full-fruited wines with classic brambly spice and enough freshness to keep elevated alcohol from dominating.

## Producer style
Pedroncelli is a longstanding family winery in Dry Creek Valley, and Mother Clone is one of its flagship Zinfandels tied to historic home-ranch plantings. The house style leans into classic Dry Creek ripeness and spice rather than ultra-modern extraction.
