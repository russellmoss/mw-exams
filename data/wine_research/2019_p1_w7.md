# Grillo, Isola della Fiamma, 2018

## Quick tasting profile
Medium lemon with ripe citrus, white peach and herbal Mediterranean notes. The palate is dry, softly textured and saline, with moderate alcohol and acidity rather than piercing sharpness. It gives the sun-lit, coastal feel typical of modern Sicilian Grillo.

## Vintage character (2018 Sicily)
Falstaff's roundup of 2018 Sicilian Grillo points to a successful vintage for fresh, saline, medium-bodied expressions of the variety. This wine fits that profile: straightforward fruit, Mediterranean herb tones and enough texture to avoid feeling simple.

## Producer style
Isola della Fiamma appears in market material as a value-driven Sicilian brand linked to western Sicilian fruit and an accessible, modern style. The emphasis is freshness, immediate drinkability and clear varietal identity rather than oak or cellar complexity.

## Why it's in this exam
This is one of the Italian pairings in 2019 Paper 1 Question 2. It gives the candidate a dry, Mediterranean island white from Grillo, contrasting both in grape and production philosophy with the lightly sparkling, sweet Moscato d'Asti that follows.
