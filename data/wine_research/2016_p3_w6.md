# Palo Cortado, Cayetano del Pino y Cia, NV

## Quick tasting profile
Amber to mahogany with walnut, orange peel, old furniture polish, dried fruit, and saline complexity. The palate is bone dry yet glycerol and expansive, combining amontillado-like aromatic finesse with oloroso-like breadth and warmth. Blind, it is unmistakably oxidative Sherry, and the interplay of concentration, acidity, and aldehydic complexity points clearly to Palo Cortado.

## Producer style
Cayetano del Pino is an historic Jerez house with renewed attention on old, age-indicated wines. The Palo Cortado sits in the serious boutique-Sherry space where long cask ageing, concentration, and authenticity matter more than volume. Commercially, it speaks to enthusiasts and fine-wine buyers rather than broad casual consumption.
