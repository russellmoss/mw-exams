# Gewürztraminer, Waimea Estates, 2014

## Quick tasting profile
Pale to medium gold; aromatic — Gewürztraminer is highly recognisable by its distinctive rose petal, lychee, ginger, and Turkish delight aromatics. Waimea's Nelson version would show: rose water, lychee, stone peach, ginger spice, possibly light smoke. Palate: medium body, slightly oily texture from the variety's high glycerol; off-dry (typical for commercial Gewürztraminer — slight RS enhances aromatic expression); moderate acidity. Clean finish. Nelson's cooler South Island climate should give more freshness than Alsace equivalents.
