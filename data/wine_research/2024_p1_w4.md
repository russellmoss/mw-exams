# Chardonnay, Yellow Tail, 2023

## Quick tasting profile
Pale to medium straw. Simple, approachable nose of melon, white peach, and light vanilla/spice undertones. The palate offers fresh peach and melon flavours with a hint of vanilla, bright acidity, and a silky smooth mouthfeel with a rich, creamy finish. Full body for the price tier. Easy-drinking commodity Chardonnay designed for broad appeal.

## Producer style
Yellow Tail, produced by Casella Family Brands in Yenda, NSW, is one of the world's best-selling wine brands. Launched in 2001, it became the fastest-growing wine brand in US history. The philosophy is extreme accessibility: simple flavours, approachable style, competitive pricing (typically $6-10 retail). The brand produces wines across all major varieties. Also appears in the corpus as Chardonnay 2017 (2018_p1_w1) and Shiraz 2021 (2023_p2_w5) — the IMW clearly uses Yellow Tail as a benchmark commodity wine.
