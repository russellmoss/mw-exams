# Riesling, Framingham, 2023

## Quick tasting profile
Pale lemon; aromatically expressive rather than austere, with mandarin, orange citrus, wet stone and a faint beeswax note. On the palate it sits in the halbtrocken/off-dry zone, with juicy acidity balancing moderate residual sugar and giving a textural, generous Marlborough take on Riesling rather than a razor-sharp Mosel model (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy; https://framingham.co.nz/products/2023-framingham-classic-riesling-case-of-six-save-40).

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Riesling (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy)
- Vineyard: Estate fruit from five Riesling blocks, picked from 20 March to 15 April (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy)
- Soils: Old riverbed-derived alluvial gravel, silt and greywacke stones are documented for nearby estate Riesling blocks, but the producer page for this bottling does not state a dedicated soil line. Source needed.
- Vinification: Hand-picked, whole-bunch pressed; mostly stainless-steel fermentation with a small portion fermented and aged in acacia barrels and sandstone vessel (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy)
- Elevage: Around five months on full ferment lees before blending and bottling (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy)
- ABV / RS / TA: 12.0% / 16-19 g/L / 7.6-8.5 g/L. Framingham’s live and archived 2023 pages show slightly different analytical figures, so both should be treated as producer-published ranges for the same release stream (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2024-framingham-classic-riesling-copy; https://framingham.co.nz/products/2023-framingham-classic-riesling-case-of-six-save-40)

## Vintage character (2023 Marlborough)
Framingham describes the 2022/23 season as warm and mostly dry through harvest, with some humid spells late in February but clean fruit and controlled picking windows thereafter (source: https://framingham.co.nz/products/2023-framingham-classic-riesling-case-of-six-save-40). That fits the wine’s ripe citrus profile and the absence of obviously green or dilute character.

## Producer style
Framingham is one of Marlborough’s established aromatic specialists and is comfortable making Riesling with texture and some sugar rather than forcing a severe dry style. The producer’s own notes emphasize estate fruit, selective hand harvest and the use of acacia/sandstone for detail rather than overt flavoring.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 2 sits between the Mosel Sekt and Mosel Beerenauslese in 2023 Paper 3, Question 1. It functions as the still-wine reference point for Riesling, forcing candidates to separate varietal markers from sweetness level and winemaking format.
