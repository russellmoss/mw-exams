# Chateau Clos de Sarpe, 2000

## Quick tasting profile
Garnet-ruby with evolved Bordeaux notes of cedar, dried blackcurrant, tobacco, earth, and truffle. The palate is dry, medium-full, and still structured, with resolved but present tannin and a mature, savory finish rather than youthful fruit.

## Vintage character (2000 Right Bank Bordeaux)
2000 is a celebrated Bordeaux vintage, combining ripeness with structure and longevity. On the Right Bank it often produced plush fruit in youth and has since moved into complex, tertiary maturity while retaining shape.

## Producer style
Clos de Sarpe is a small, serious Saint-Emilion estate, and the wine reads as classic claret rather than a modern international red. The emphasis is on mature complexity, terroir, and bottle evolution.
