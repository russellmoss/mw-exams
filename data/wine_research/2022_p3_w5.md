# Gewürztraminer, Single Vineyard, Te Whare Ra Wines, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
Medium gold with a slight copper tinge (typical for Gewurztraminer); intensely perfumed and aromatic nose of lychee, rose petal, Turkish delight, ginger, cinnamon and exotic spice — Gewurztraminer's unmistakably distinctive aromatic signature. Full-bodied for the ABV (14%), with the variety's characteristic oily, viscous texture; the slight off-dry balance cuts the intensity of the aromatics; warm, spicy mid-palate; medium-long finish. New Zealand Gewurztraminer at 14% typically shows more exuberant and ripe fruit than Alsace equivalents.

## Producer style
Te Whare Ra is one of Marlborough's older boutique estates, established 1979, now under ownership of Jason and Anna Flowerday. The estate is known for biodynamic or organic viticulture and focuses on aromatic varieties — Gewurztraminer, Riesling, Pinot Gris — alongside Pinot Noir. Te Whare Ra is among the handful of New Zealand producers making serious single-vineyard Gewurztraminer at high quality. Source needed for specific estate details and biodynamic certification.
