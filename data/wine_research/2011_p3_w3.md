# Verdelho 15 Years Old, Henriques and Henriques

## Quick tasting profile
Expect lifted acidity, oxidative nuttiness, citrus peel, caramel and a sweet-savoury finish. Verdelho Madeira sits between the drier Sercial model and the richer Bual/Malmsey register, so balance between acidity and sweetness is central.
