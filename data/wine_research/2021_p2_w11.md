# La Montesa Crianza, Palacios Remondo, 2017

## Quick tasting profile
Medium ruby with red cherry, strawberry, dried thyme and sweet spice. The palate is dry, medium-bodied and supple, with Garnacha-led fruit, moderate tannin and an elegant rather than heavily oaked Rioja frame. It sits on the fresher, more lifted side of Rioja.

## Vintage character (2017 Rioja)
Merchant notes on the 2017 bottling describe a warm, dry year with very early harvest timing. That points to a generous-fruited but still fragrant style, especially in Garnacha from altitude-influenced Rioja Oriental.

## Producer style
Palacios Remondo, under the wider Álvaro Palacios orbit, is associated with Garnacha-led Rioja of greater finesse and site expression than old-school American-oak stereotypes. La Montesa is the estate's key mid-tier expression and has strong commercial appeal.

## Why it's in this exam
This is the first of the Rioja pair in 2021 Paper 2 Question 4. It offers a contemporary, Garnacha-led Crianza profile to contrast with the grandly traditional, long-aged Castillo Ygay.
