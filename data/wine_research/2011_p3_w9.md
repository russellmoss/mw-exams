# Wehlener Sonnenuhr Kabinett, Joh. Jos. Prüm

## Quick tasting profile
Classic Mosel Kabinett should show featherweight alcohol, slatey citrus fruit and delicately balanced residual sugar. The key is levity and precision rather than density.
