# Shiraz, Yellow Tail, 2021

## Quick tasting profile
Deep ruby. Straightforward, fruit-led nose of plum, red cherry, vanilla, mocha and sweet spice. The palate is smooth, soft and commercially polished rather than terroir-driven, with moderate tannin, easy acidity and a broad, juicy finish.

## Producer style
Yellow Tail is the global, high-volume Casella family brand based in Yenda, New South Wales. The stated aim is accessible, easy-to-understand wine with broad appeal, and the style here fits that brief exactly: ripe fruit, soft texture, obvious flavour cues and dependable price-point consistency rather than complexity.
