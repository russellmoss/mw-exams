# Vintage Port, Fonseca Guimaraens

## Quick tasting profile
Expect concentrated dark fruit, spirit, tannin and youthful-to-mature bottle complexity rather than overt oxidative nut character. Vintage Port should look denser and more structured than the tawny pair-mate.
