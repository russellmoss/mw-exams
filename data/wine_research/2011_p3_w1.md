# Comtes de Champagne Blanc de Blancs, Taittinger

## Quick tasting profile
Mature prestige Blanc de Blancs Champagne should show fine mousse, lemon curd, toast, chalk and a long autolytic finish. The palate is dry, precise and layered, with more persistence and complexity than a standard non-vintage brut.
