# Albarino La Trucha, Bodegas Notas Frutales, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
Expect a bright Atlantic white with citrus, peach skin, white flowers and a saline edge rather than overt perfume. The palate should be dry, crisp and medium-bodied, with enough lees texture to avoid feeling dilute but no oak influence.

## Vintage character (2020 Rias Baixas)
2020 in Atlantic Spain generally gave vivid aromatics and good acidity where fruit stayed healthy. In Rias Baixas, the better wines balance sea-spray freshness and stone-fruit generosity rather than leaning too sharply into green notes.

## Producer style
La Trucha is marketed as a focused Albarino brand from Rias Baixas, aimed at clear varietal expression and broad export appeal. The style is intentionally legible and modern: fresh, saline and approachable, but still rooted in the Atlantic identity of Galicia.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 10 is the Spanish member of the four-wine single-variety white set in 2022 Paper 1 Question 4. It gives candidates a classic premium-commercial Albarino benchmark and tests whether they can separate Atlantic salinity and texture from the more floral Torrontes, peppery Gruner and richer Rhone Roussanne.
