# Prosecco Rosé Brut, La Gioiosa, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
Very pale salmon-pink to light copper with fine, persistent bubbles (Charmat-produced); fresh and fruity nose of strawberry, white peach, apple, rose petal and light citrus — the characteristic fresh, primary Prosecco aromatic register with a rosé twist. Light-bodied (11% ABV), brut dryness, refreshing acidity and clean finish; minimal autolytic development (Charmat method). Produced under the relatively new Prosecco Rosé DOC (approved 2020), this wine represents the contemporary Italian rosé sparkling category.

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Minimum 85% Glera + minimum 10-15% Pinot Nero (the Pinot Noir component provides the rosé colour and vinous structure)
- Vineyard: Veneto and Friuli-Venezia Giulia; tank production
- Vinification: Charmat-Martinotti method (secondary fermentation in pressurised tanks); no bottle fermentation
- Elevage: Short tank maturation; early disgorgement and bottling for freshness
- Dosage: Brut (approx 6-12 g/L RS) — Source needed for exact La Gioiosa dosage
- ABV / RS / TA: 11% / brut / medium-high acidity
- Prosecco DOC Rosé was officially approved in 2020 (Source: common knowledge)

## Vintage character (2020 Veneto)
2020 was a vintaged Prosecco Rosé DOC year — the first full vintage of the Prosecco Rosé designation. The 2020 growing season in Veneto was generally good with ripe fruit and adequate acidity. For Charmat-method wine, vintage character is secondary to production method freshness.
