# Billette Rosé, Pradel, 2015. Provence, France

## Quick tasting profile
Pale onion-skin or salmon colour (Provence's signature pale pink), brilliant clarity. Nose of fresh red fruit (strawberry, cherry), white peach, dried herbs (garrigue), and a subtle mineral note; restrained, elegant aromatic profile. Dry on the palate (bone dry by Provence convention), crisp acidity, light-medium body, short-to-medium clean finish. The defining contrast to Sutter Home White Zinfandel: both are pink wines, but this is bone dry vs. notably sweet.

2015 was a warm, dry vintage in Provence — conditions that suit rosé production, producing ripe, aromatic wines with good texture. The vintage delivered generous flavour with maintained freshness. 2015 Provence rosés were generally noted for their generous fruit character.
