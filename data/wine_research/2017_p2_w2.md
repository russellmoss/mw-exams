# Cabernet Sauvignon / Shiraz, Oxford Landing, 2014. South Australia, Australia

## Quick tasting profile
Medium ruby-purple, youthful. Nose of ripe blackcurrant, blackberry, plum, with lifted eucalyptus and a light vanillin oak note. Medium-full bodied, ripe soft tannins, low-medium acidity, juicy fruit-forward palate with a short-to-medium finish. Approachable and commercial in style — designed for early drinking rather than ageing. The Cabernet/Shiraz blend is a classic Australian commercial pairing.
