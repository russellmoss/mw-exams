# Riesling Sekt Extra Dry, Dr. Loosen, NV

## Quick tasting profile
Pale lemon with a fine, energetic mousse. Blind, this should read as cool-climate bottle-fermented Riesling sparkling wine: green apple, citrus, white flowers and wet stone, with a brisk spine of acidity and a lightly softening touch of dosage rather than full Brut Nature severity (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf).

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: 100% Riesling (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf)
- Vineyard: Estate vineyards in the Middle Mosel (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wine/dr-loosen-riesling-sekt-extra-dry/)
- Soils: Mosel slate influence is stated stylistically, but exact parcel soils for this bottling were not published. Source needed.
- Vinification: Traditional bottle fermentation (`Flaschengärung`), with clarification by transfer method and dosage at re-bottling (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf)
- Elevage: 15 months on lees in bottle (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf)
- ABV / RS / TA: 12.0% / 11.9 g/L / 6.4 g/L. The exam corpus lists 11.5%, so that discrepancy should be noted rather than normalized away (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf)

## Vintage character (NV / category character)
This is non-vintage Sekt, so base-wine year matters less than house style. The key exam-relevant point is that bottle-fermented Riesling from the Mosel keeps high acidity and aromatic precision, giving a sparkling wine that sits stylistically between Prosecco-like fruitiness and Champagne-method autolysis.

## Producer style
Dr. Loosen is one of the benchmark Mosel estates, focused on old vines, harvest selection and gentle cellar work under Ernst Loosen (source: https://loosenbrosusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Dr-Loosen-Riesling-Sekt-Extra-Dry-Info.pdf). Even in an entry sparkling wine, the stylistic through-line is clarity of fruit, bright acidity and transparent regional identity rather than heavy winemaking imprint.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 1 opens 2023 Paper 3 as one of three Riesling-based wines spanning sparkling, still and sweet styles. It tests whether the candidate can identify Riesling under bottle-fermented conditions and discuss traditional-method sparkling winemaking outside Champagne.
