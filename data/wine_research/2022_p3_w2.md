# Spumante Rosato "Cadelvento", Venturini Baldini, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
Ruby-pink to light crimson with lively, dense bubbles; fresh and fruity nose of red berries, raspberry, strawberry and violet — Lambrusco's characteristic fragrant red-fruit register. Light-bodied (11.5% ABV), bright, refreshing palate with high acidity and a clean, dry or off-dry finish (Brut/Extra Dry style); slight earthy/mineral note typical of quality Lambrusco. A premium expression of Lambrusco Spumante Rosato that contrasts with the commercial sweet style historically associated with the variety.

## Technical specs
- Grape variety: Lambrusco grape varieties (likely Lambrusco Grasparossa, Lambrusco Salamino or Lambrusco Sorbara — Reggiano DOC blends multiple Lambrusco clones)
- Vineyard: Emilia-Romagna, Reggio Emilia area; clay-limestone alluvial soils of the Po Valley
- Vinification: Charmat (tank) method for secondary fermentation — standard for Lambrusco production
- Elevage: Short tank conditioning; early bottling for freshness
- ABV / RS / TA: 11.5% / brut or extra dry / high acidity
- Cadelvento = estate/cuvée name for Venturini Baldini's premium Lambrusco

## Vintage character (2020 Emilia-Romagna)
2020 was a good growing season in Emilia-Romagna with warm conditions producing ripe Lambrusco fruit. For Charmat-method Lambrusco, vintage character is less decisive than production quality.
