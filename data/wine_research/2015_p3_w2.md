# Rosé NV, Knightor Vineyard, Cornwall

## Quick tasting profile
Pale salmon-pink; delicate mousse suggesting traditional method or Charmat. Nose: redcurrant, strawberry, light yeasty notes given low ABV (11.5%). Palate: fresh, light-bodied, modest fruit concentration, elevated acidity typical of cool Cornwall climate. Finish short to medium. The low alcohol and English provenance are the defining blind signals.
