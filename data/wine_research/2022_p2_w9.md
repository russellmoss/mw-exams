# Malbec Serie A, Zuccardi, 2020

## Quick tasting profile
This should show juicy black fruit, violet, light herbs and supple tannin with fresher lift than a heavily oaked old-vine Mendoza style. The profile is modern Uco Valley Malbec: ripe, polished and commercially attractive, but not overbuilt.

## Vintage character (2020 Uco Valley)
2020 was a strong Argentine vintage in many regions, with healthy fruit and enough freshness in higher-altitude Mendoza sites. In Uco Valley Malbec that generally means vivid fruit, good color and a balance between richness and floral lift.

## Producer style
The Zuccardi Serie A line is designed to showcase Argentina's regional diversity in a polished, accessible format. Compared with more site-specific or old-vine Malbec, this bottling typically prioritizes clarity, freshness and wide market appeal.

## Why it's in this exam
Wine 9 begins 2022 Paper 2 Question 4, a same-variety, same-region Malbec comparison. It is the more broadly cast Uco Valley Malbec whose job is to be contrasted with the denser, older-vine Altocedro wine on style, quality and market potential.
