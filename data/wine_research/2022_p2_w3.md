# Merlot, Vino de Eyzaguirre, 2019

## Quick tasting profile
Expect soft, fruit-forward Merlot with plum, blueberry, blackcurrant and a touch of pepper or dried herbs. Compared with the Chinon and Napa Cabernet, this should feel gentler in tannin, rounder in texture and more straightforwardly commercial.

## Vintage character (2019 Colchagua)
Colchagua is generally well suited to ripe, supple reds, and 2019 again favored generous fruit concentration in much of Chile. For entry-to-mid-tier Merlot, the result is usually immediate drinkability rather than angular structure or strong tertiary complexity.

## Producer style
Vino de Eyzaguirre is positioned as an accessible Chilean brand rather than a boutique icon estate. That makes the wine useful pedagogically: it gives the set a clean, varietally legible Merlot whose commercial style contrasts sharply with the more site-specific Chinon and prestige Napa Cabernet.
