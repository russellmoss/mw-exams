# Saumur-Champigny, Le Prince, Domaine de Rocheville, 2014. Loire, France

## Quick tasting profile
Medium ruby-purple with violet tinge at youthful edge. Nose of bright red fruits — raspberry, redcurrant, Morello cherry — with characteristic Cabernet Franc freshness: green pepper, pencil shavings, herbs and flint. Palate is medium-bodied with fine, grippy tannins, lively acidity and a fruit-forward, mineral finish. Classic cool-climate Cab Franc; light and elegant rather than heavy.

## Vintage character (2014 Loire)
2014 in the Loire was one of the better vintages of the decade: warm summer, late harvest in excellent conditions, yielding wines with good ripeness balanced by the Loire's natural acidity. Saumur-Champigny 2014s are considered approachable and fruit-expressive, with good varietal definition. An accessible, pleasant vintage rather than a great one.
