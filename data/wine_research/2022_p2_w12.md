# Lytton Springs, Ridge Vineyards, 2019

## Quick tasting profile
This is a more complex, structured Dry Creek wine than the Pedroncelli: brambly black and red fruit, cracked pepper, herbs, earth and American-oak seasoning over firmer tannin and longer finish. The field-blend makeup adds more complexity and savory nuance than a straight varietal Zinfandel.

## Vintage character (2019 Dry Creek Valley)
2019 in Sonoma produced complete, energetic reds with ripe tannin and good balance. For Lytton Springs, that generally means full flavor intensity but with enough freshness and structure to support medium-term ageing.

## Producer style
Ridge is one of California's benchmark traditionalist wineries, known for transparent ingredient labeling, restrained cellar intervention and deep respect for old-vine field blends. Lytton Springs is an iconic Dry Creek bottling whose quality and reputation sit above most straightforward varietal Zinfandels.
