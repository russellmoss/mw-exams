# Homage, Trinity Hill, 2014

## Quick tasting profile
Deeper and more concentrated than the Te Mata Syrah, with cassis, smoked meat, violet, black olive, and toasted spice. The palate is dry, full, and structured, with more oak, more extraction, and greater cellar potential than the regional counterpart.

## Vintage character (2014 Hawke's Bay)
2014 in Hawke's Bay was warm and favorable, giving ripe fruit and strong concentration. The best Syrahs from the Gravels show both generosity and the peppery, savory line that keeps the wines from becoming monolithic.

## Producer style
Trinity Hill's Homage is a flagship New Zealand Syrah, modeled more on serious, savory fine wine than on mass-market Shiraz. It is a statement wine built for depth, oak integration, and ageability.
