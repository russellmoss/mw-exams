# Cote Rotie La Landonne, Delas Freres, 2012

## Quick tasting profile
Deep ruby with smoked meat, violet, black olive, cassis, and cracked pepper. The palate is dry, concentrated, and tightly structured, with Syrah tannin, savory reduction, and oak spice sitting over a long mineral, ferrous finish.

## Vintage character (2012 Northern Rhone)
2012 was a small but often excellent Northern Rhone vintage, yielding concentrated wines with sound balance. In top Cote Rotie sites it can give serious structure and aromatic lift without the excess weight of hotter years.

## Producer style
Delas Freres operates in a polished, premium Northern Rhone register, aiming for seriousness and cellarworthiness. La Landonne is expected to emphasize site nobility and density rather than early accessibility.
