# Castillo Ygay Gran Reserva, Marques de Murrieta, 2010

## Quick tasting profile
Deep garnet-ruby with mature black cherry, cedar, tobacco, dried flowers and fine spice. The palate is dry, concentrated and seamless, with integrated tannins, savoury complexity and long, aristocratic length. This is classic long-aged Rioja at the highest level rather than a simple crianza-style red.

## Vintage character (2010 Rioja)
The long-release nature of Castillo Ygay means the wine expresses both the growing season and extensive cellar ageing. The 2010 bottling is widely treated in the market as an outstanding, ageworthy release, with enough freshness to carry the extended maturation.

## Producer style
Marqués de Murrieta is one of Rioja's historic reference houses, and Castillo Ygay is its flagship traditional luxury wine. The style is defined by rigorous selection, long élevage and a balance between mature savoury complexity and polished fruit.
