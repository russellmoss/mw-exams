## IMW's likely intent

## What to focus on in the glass
- Bubble size and persistence: fine/persistent = traditional method; coarser/shorter = tank method
- Autolytic character: brioche, biscuit, toast = extended lees aging (traditional method)
- Fruit character: apple/citrus = cooler climate; peach/pear = Prosecco or warmer; floral = Moscato/Asti
- Dosage: bone dry = brut nature/zero dosage; hint of sweetness = brut/extra dry
- Color: deeper gold = aged/vintage; pale straw = young NV
- Mousse texture: creamy = traditional method with extended aging; frothy = tank method
- Oxidative vs reductive: honey/nut = older traditional; fresh/bright = young/tank
