## IMW's likely intent
This is a benchmark-sweet-wine comparison. The examiner wants the candidate to distinguish botrytized sweet wines that can look superficially similar in richness but arise from different grapes, harvest regimes, and cellar handling. The best answers will discuss noble rot, berry selection, must concentration, fermentation management, oak aging, and how those choices create different balances of sweetness, acidity, and development.
