## IMW's likely intent

The 16-mark production method comparison is the exam's center of gravity. The candidate must articulate the specific decisions (botrytis selection, oxidative ageing, arrested fermentation, flor, etc.) that create each style.

## What to focus on in the glass
- **Colour contrast**: if one is golden/amber and the other pale, that signals different production/ageing
- **Sweetness**: one dry, one sweet? Both dry but different oxidation levels?
- **Oxidative character**: nutty, dried fruit, toffee = oxidative ageing or flor
- **Botrytis markers**: honey, marmalade, apricot, saffron
- **Alcohol level**: fortified (18-20%+), sweet (7-12%), dry table (12-14%), oxidative (14-16%)
- **Acidity**: high acid with sweetness = Germanic or Tokaj; high acid with oxidation = Jura or Sherry
