## IMW's likely intent

The IMW wants to see: (1) that the candidate can identify the variety blind, (2) that the candidate can articulate how the same grape tastes different in cool vs warm climates (fruit spectrum, tannin structure, acidity, alcohol), and (3) that the candidate understands how winemakers adapt techniques to climate (extraction time, oak regime, whole-bunch inclusion, co-fermentation, acid adjustment). The heavy weighting toward climate/winemaking commentary (45/75 marks) means this question rewards analytical depth over identification precision.
