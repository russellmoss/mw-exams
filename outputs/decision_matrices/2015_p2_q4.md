## What to focus on in the glass

- Fruit profile: cassis/blackcurrant = Cab; plum/blueberry = Merlot; dark cherry/tobacco = Cab Franc
- Body and weight: Cab is typically more angular, Merlot more round
- Oak: American oak (coconut/dill/vanilla) points to New World or Rioja; French oak (cedar/clove) to Bordeaux or premium New World
- Alcohol: Bordeaux typically 13-14%, Napa 14-15%+
- Ripeness: opulent ripe fruit = Napa/Barossa; more restrained = Bordeaux/Stellenbosch
- Acidity: moderate-high = Bordeaux; moderate = Napa
