## IMW's likely intent
The candidate is being tested on maturity judgment under subtle conditions. It is not enough to say one wine is older; the answer should connect developmental stage to structural reserves and appellation level. If one wine is from a superior site, it may both appear less evolved and have more runway despite similar or even older vintage.

## What to focus on in the glass
- Color rim evolution is crucial for vintage estimation
- Compare fruit freshness versus sous-bois, mushroom, leather, dried flower, and spice
- Tannin resolution and acid line will indicate whether the wine is plateauing or still rising
- Quality should be discussed in regional context: concentration, balance, site expression, length
- Potential to improve must be tied to structure, not prestige alone
