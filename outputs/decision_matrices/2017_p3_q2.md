## Candidate styles (confidence tiers)

- **Oxidative white**: **PLAUSIBLE** -- Vin Jaune style or solera-aged
- **Skin-contact rose**: **CURVEBALL** -- extended maceration on red grapes producing something between rose and light red

## IMW's likely intent

The 2017 exam would have been at the height of the orange wine/skin-contact trend. The IMW wants to see if candidates can articulate: extended skin maceration, use of amphora/qvevri or other vessels, wild fermentation, minimal sulfur, and how these techniques affect color, tannin, and aromatics. The market positioning question tests whether the candidate understands the niche/natural wine market.

## What to focus on in the glass

- Tannin: if present in what appears to be a white wine, it's skin-contact
- Oxidative character: bruised apple, dried fruit, aldehydic notes
