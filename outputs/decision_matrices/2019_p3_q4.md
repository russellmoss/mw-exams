## IMW's likely intent

This is a core MW topic — the comparison between Sherry's solera system with continuous flor and Jura's static barrel aging under voile. ## What to focus on in the glass
- **Flor/biological character**: yeasty, chamomile, almond, saline, dried herbs
- **Color**: pale straw/greenish (Fino/Manzanilla) vs. deeper gold (Vin Jaune or older oxidative)
- **Salinity and dryness**: bone-dry, saline = Manzanilla; waxy, nutty, oxidative = Vin Jaune
- **Alcohol**: 15-15.5% for Sherry (fortified); 13-14.5% for Jura (naturally high from evaporation)
- **Texture**: sharp and angular (Manzanilla) vs. broader and more oxidative (Vin Jaune)
- **Aldehydes**: acetaldehydes from flor aging
