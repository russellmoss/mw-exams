## c) Discuss the method of production.
Wine 5
lagares (foot-treading) or autovinification for extraction; fortified with grape spirit (aguardente) to arrest fermentation at ~20% ABV  maximum 2.5 years in wood before bottling per regulations; then long bottle aging
These are the decisive production choices because they explain the fortified, sweet, full rich, neutral old style.

These are the decisive production choices because they explain the fortified, very sweet, full rich, neutral old style.
Wine 8

These are the decisive production choices because they explain the fortified, medium, full rich, neutral old style.
