## Anti-overlink note
The temptation to over-link three traditional-method sparklings into a single "all Champagne" narrative is the trap. The third slot is almost always a New World traditional-method wine; identifying it requires noting the riper fruit profile and less mineral tension. The matrix should write three different *quality stories* (NV commercial / vintage prestige / NW premium), not a single Champagne-tier ladder.

Lesson:

Roederer Estate's Champagne-ownership specifically explains why W3 is harder than expected.
