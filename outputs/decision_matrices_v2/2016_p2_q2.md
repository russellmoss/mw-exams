## What to focus on in the glass (family-pack guided)

- Climate discrimination: cool-continental (Germany) = more austere, angular, herbal/earthy; cool-maritime (NZ) = pure red fruit, freshness; moderate-maritime (Australia) = broader, more ripe cherry/plum.
