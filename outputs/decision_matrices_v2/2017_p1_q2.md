## Anti-collapse note

The maturity contrast is the most visible in-glass signal: W4's amber tones and tertiary development vs W5's pale-gold youth. Do not let the maturity difference mislead into thinking the varieties differ — this is the F1 same-variety structure, and maturity is part of the comparative axis. The winemaking sub-question (5 marks per wine) should focus on the specific choices that explain each wine's character: traditional foudre vs stainless, ageing length, RS management, harvest date.
