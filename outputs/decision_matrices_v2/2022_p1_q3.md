## Anti-collapse note

The "markets" sub-question is structured around Italy's deliberately contrasting commercial positions. Do not assign similar market descriptions to all three. The defining contrast is:
- W6 (Pinot Grigio): mass supermarket, high volume, commodity pricing
- W7 (Gewurztraminer): specialist independent retail, aromatic niche, premium per-bottle pricing
- W8 (Etna Bianco): on-trade fine-dining, emerging category, fashion-driven, highest per-bottle premium

Conflating W6 and W8 as "both Italian whites with similar markets" wastes the market differentiation that earns marks. The "variety/ies" plural hint is worth noting: Etna Bianco di Sei may contain Catarratto alongside Carricante, making it technically a blend.
